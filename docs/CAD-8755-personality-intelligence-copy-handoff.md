# CAD-8755 Personality Intelligence Copy Handoff

## Narrative Spine

**Named enemy:** the remote-contributor management chasm. Distributed managers cannot reliably see how people prefer to communicate, process feedback, recover from friction, or collaborate across distance.

**Positioning shift:** do not frame this as an assessment or test. Frame it as **Personality Intelligence**: a consented signal layer that helps people understand how they work, then makes that context useful in 1:1s, coaching, and talent conversations as integrations ship.

**Customer-as-hero framing:** People leaders and managers are trying to make better conversations happen. Cadence is the guide that turns working-style signal into context, not judgment.

## Marketing-Truth Boundaries

| Claim area | Allowed now | Roadmap-labeled until shipped | Never claim |

**Long version:** Raw OCEAN responses do not cross the module boundary. Cadence derives working-style signal for profile, team, and coaching views. Personality signal never feeds ratings, compensation, 9-box placement, or performance scores.
